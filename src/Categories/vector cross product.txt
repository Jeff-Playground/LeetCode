Two vectors' cross product computes a third vector which is perpendicular to the two vectors. The value of the new vector
is a*b*sinθ, if this value is positive, then θ is less than 180º, otherwise it's larger than 180º.
One of the common use cases is to determine whether a polygon is convex.
