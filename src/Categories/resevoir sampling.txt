When sampling k items, first get the first k items, then for the n-th item, the possibility for including it is k/n, and
if included, randomly replace one of the chosen k items:
Random r=new Random();
int idx=r.nextInt(n);
if(idx<k){
    int temp=nums[idx];
    nums[idx]=nums[n-1];
    nums[n-1]=temp;
}
